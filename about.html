Who We Are

Bajrangbali Shaikshnik Evam Samajik Seva Sansthan is a grassroots, not-for-profit organization committed to creating equal access to quality education for children and communities from underserved backgrounds. Our journey began with a simple belief: every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and thrive — regardless of their socio-economic status. We work across rural and urban marginalized areas in India to provide not just academic support, but a nurturing ecosystem for holistic development.



Our Vision

To build a future where every child has the opportunity to learn with dignity, dream without fear, and develop the skills they need to lead empowered lives.



Our Values

Equity – We believe education is a right, not a privilege.
Empowerment – We support children and youth to become change agents.
Integrity – We are transparent, accountable, and community-driven.
Inclusion – We celebrate diversity and work across gender, caste, and region.
Sustainability – We build long-term programs with local ownership.


What Makes Us Different

  • ➤ We combine education with empathy, academic help with emotional support.
  • ➤ Our solutions are hyper-local, customized to each community’s needs.
  • ➤ We actively involve parents, teachers, and youth to build ownership.
  • ➤ We track impact with transparency and data-backed outcomes.
